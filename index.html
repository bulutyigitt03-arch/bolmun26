Conference Schedule

Three days of
diplomacy

June 13–15, 2026 · Gülser Mehmet Bolluk Anatolian High School, Balıkesir

Friday — June 13, 2026
Opening Ceremony & First Committee Session
Delegates register and attend the grand opening ceremony followed by the first formal committee session. Opening address by the Director-General and keynote speaker.
Saturday — June 14, 2026
Full Committee Sessions & Social Night
A full day of debate, caucusing, and resolution drafting. Evening social event organized by the Entertainment team for delegates to connect.
Sunday — June 15, 2026
Resolution Voting & Closing Ceremony
Final voting procedure on all resolutions, followed by the awards ceremony — Best Delegate, Outstanding Delegate, and Honorable Mention in each committee.
