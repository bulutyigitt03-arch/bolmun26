FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is BollukMUN 2026? +

BollukMUN 2026 will take place on June 13–15, 2026 at Gülser Mehmet Bolluk Anatolian High School in Balıkesir.

Do I need prior MUN experience to apply? +

Not at all! BollukMUN 2026 is open to all experience levels. We offer an orientation session for first-time delegates.

Is there a registration fee? +

Please contact us for registration fee information. Scholarship opportunities are available.

What language will the sessions be held in? +

Sessions will be conducted in English. Turkish support may be available for specific committees.

Is accommodation provided? +

Accommodation can be arranged for delegates from nearby schools. Details will be shared after acceptance.
